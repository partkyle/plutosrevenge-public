Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how "Pluto's Revenge" ("the Game") handles information when you use our mobile application.

We do not collect any personal data from you.

Information We Collect

The Game is designed to be played offline and does not connect to any external servers. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or device information from our users.

Local Storage of Scores

The Game may store your high scores or game progress locally on your device. This data is stored only on your device and is not accessible by us or any third parties. If you uninstall the Game, this locally stored data will be removed.

No Third-Party Access

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services within the Game. Your gameplay and any locally stored scores are entirely private to your device.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As we do not collect any data, we adhere to the principles of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Game or on our website (if applicable). You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
